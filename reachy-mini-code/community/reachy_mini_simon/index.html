Three Difficulty Modes

1

Head Only

4 Directions

Classic Simon - just head tilts (Up, Down, Left, Right)

Left antenna: Straight up (0°)
2

Head + Body

6 Directions

Head tilts + body yaw rotation (adds Body Left, Body Right)

Left antenna: ~45°
3

Full Robot

10 Directions!

Head + body + antenna movements (4 antenna directions)

Left antenna: ~90°

How to Play

1

Select Difficulty

Move the LEFT antenna to choose difficulty (1, 2, or 3)

2

Start the Game

Pull the RIGHT antenna to start (it twitches when ready!)

3

Watch the Sequence

Reachy demonstrates with head, body, and/or antennas

4

Repeat & Win!

Repeat the sequence - each round adds one more move!
