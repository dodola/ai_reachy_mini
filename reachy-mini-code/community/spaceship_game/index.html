Spaceship Game

Defend the galaxy with Reachy Mini's head movements and antennas!

🎮 GAMEPLAY 🎮

Control a spaceship in 3D space
Fight waves of enemy ships
Dodge bullets and aim precisely
Score points and survive!

🤖 CONTROLS 🤖

🎯 Game Features

🤖

Physical Control

Move Reachy Mini's head to aim your spaceship in 3D space

💥

Dual Weapons

Pull left and right antennas to fire your guns independently

👾

Enemy Waves

Face increasingly difficult waves with three enemy types

🎯

Bullet Patterns

Dodge complex bullet patterns from fast, heavy, and aimed enemies

🕹️ How to Play

⬆️⬇️
Tilt Head Up/Down Aim your spaceship up and down
⬅️➡️
Turn Head Left/Right Aim your spaceship left and right
🔄
Tilt Head Sideways Visual banking effect (for immersion)
📡
Pull Antennas Down Fire your left and right guns - pull to shoot!

🎮 Enemy Types

🔴

Red Enemies (100pts)

Basic enemies that shoot straight bullets. Good for practice!

🟠

Orange Enemies (150pts)

Fast movers with aimed shots. They track your position!

🟣

Purple Enemies (300pts)

Heavy ships with spread shot patterns. Tough but rewarding!
