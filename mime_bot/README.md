# Mime Bot

Open this Space, sign in with Hugging Face, pick your Reachy Mini, and the
robot will mimic your face live from your webcam (or phone front camera).

A free-routing tuner lets you map any face expression (~52 ARKit-style
blendshapes from MediaPipe) to the robot's antennas and body — head
orientation and depth are mapped directly from your face's 6D pose, with
amplitude sliders.

No recording, no upload, no setup beyond opening the URL.
